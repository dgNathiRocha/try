Map Chart

Geographic maps are used as a visual backdrop in map charts, which are graphical representations that show data points or statistical information related to certain geographic places. To illustrate data trends, patterns, or correlations across several locations or coordinates, these charts make use of a variety of graphic components, including colors, symbols, and shading.
