Box Chart

A box and whisker plot, sometimes referred to as a box plot, shows a collection of data's five-number summary. The minimum, first quartile, median, third quartile, and maximum make up the five-number summary. A box is drawn in a box plot from the first to the third quartile. At the median, a vertical line passes through the box. The whiskers extend to the minimum or maximum at each quartile.
